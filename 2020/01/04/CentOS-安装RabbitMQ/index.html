CentOS 安装RabbitMQ

2. 安装RabbitMQ

rpm -Uvh http://www.rabbitmq.com/releases/rabbitmq-server/v3.5.6/rabbitmq-server-3.5.6-1.noarch.rpm

安装之后 使用rpm -qa|grep rabbitmq查看是否OK
